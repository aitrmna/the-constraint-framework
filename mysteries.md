<h2>The Method</h2>

<p><strong>Every case follows the same process:</strong></p>

<ol>
  <li>Define the mystery</li>
  <li>List all serious hypotheses</li>
  <li>Generate predictions for each</li>
  <li>Check predictions against reality</li>
  <li>Score confirmations vs failures</li>
  <li>Weight severity of failures</li>
  <li>Compare hypotheses numerically</li>
</ol>
