### CONSTRAINT 1 (MOST DAMNING): Weapons Systems Disabled

**Evidence:**
- First F-4 (pilot Yousefi): All instrumentation and communications went dead when approaching the object. Systems restored when he turned away.
- Second F-4 (pilot Jafari): Attempted to fire AIM-9 missile at a smaller object that detached. Weapons control panel and communications went dead. Systems restored when object moved away.

**What this means:** Two separate aircraft, two separate pilots, same effect — systems disabled on approach, restored on retreat. This is not random malfunction.

**ELIMINATES:**
- ❌ Star/planet (doesn't disable weapons systems)
- ❌ Normal aircraft (doesn't disable weapons systems)
- ❌ Meteor (doesn't disable weapons systems)
- ❌ Random equipment failure (too precisely correlated with approach/retreat)

**Remaining:** Deliberate electronic warfare OR non-human technology

---

### CONSTRAINT 2: Multiple Radar Confirmations

**Evidence:**
- Ground radar at Mehrabad Airport tracked the object
- Both F-4s got radar locks on the object
- Object showed on radar as solid return, not weather or noise

**ELIMINATES:**
- ❌ Optical illusion (radar confirmed physical object)
- ❌ Atmospheric phenomenon (solid radar return)
- ❌ Mass hallucination (instrumentation confirmed)

**Remaining:** Physical object with electronic warfare capability

---

### CONSTRAINT 3: Visual Descriptions — Multiple Witnesses

**Evidence:**
- Civilian witnesses reported bright object before military involvement
- Tower controllers saw it
- Both F-4 pilots described: Intensely bright, flashing strobing lights (red, green, blue, orange), object changed position rapidly
- Second pilot described smaller object detaching and approaching his aircraft at high speed

**ELIMINATES:**
- ❌ Single witness error (multiple independent observers)
- ❌ Misidentified Jupiter (Jupiter doesn't strobe colors, detach objects, or approach aircraft)

**Remaining:** Physical object with unusual light characteristics and ability to deploy smaller objects
